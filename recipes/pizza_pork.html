Pizza Pork

The boneless and roasted pork with the classic pizza filling on top. Very tasty. That melted cheese makes your mouth water.

Ingredients

  • 1 pork
  • 3 slices of smoked calabreza sausage
  • 3 slices of sausage paio
  • 1 tube of cowpea
  • 1 cup of cottage cheese
  • 300 gr of mozzarella cheese
  • 300 gr of ham
  • 1 black olive glass
  • 1 green olive glass
  • 2 glasses of chopped hearts of palm
  • 300 Gr of bacon
  • 200 gr of parmesan cheese
  • oregano
  • 1 can of green corn
  • 1 kg of tomato
  • 0.5 kg of onion
  • 1 can of tomato sauce

Method of preparation

  1. Place the open deboned pig upside down with the leather facing up to roast in an oven at a high temperature for an average of 6 hours. Then turn the leather face down and assemble the stuffing.
  2. First pass the tomato sauce over the meat; then add the sausages, bacon, tomato, onion, heart of palm, corn and olives (keep some to put on top, as decoration), the cream cheese and curd, and lastly the ham and cover with the mozzarella and sprinkle with the grated Parmesan.
  3. After filling, return the pork to the oven, and roast for another 1 hour and a half.
  4. Tip: once ready, to serve, cut the leather with the filling with scissors.
