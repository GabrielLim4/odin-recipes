Coxinha Cake

Coxinha Cake, learn with this super easy and simple recipe how to make a simple and easy savory cake, made with the most beloved savory cake in Brazil, this delicacy that makes your mouth water just thinking, How about going from Coxinha Cake to one family snack.

Ingredients

filling

  • 1 kg of chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoons of salt dessert of salt
  • 1 chicken broth tablet
  • Water to cover the chicken
  • Garlic onion, coloral and eye to taste (for sauteing)
  • Parsley and chive

pasta

  • 1 kg of potatoes
  • 1 chicken broth tablet
  • 1 dessert spoons of salt
  • 1/2 glass of milk
  • 2 half cup of water water left over from cooking the chicken
  • 700 grams of wheat flour to give the point
  • 1 tablespoons of margarine

to bread

  • Water amount to cover the coxinha
  • 2 tablespoons of wheat flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

chicken stuffing

  1. To make the filling, place the chicken breast with a tablet of chicken stock and water in a pressure cooker.
  2. Then, shred the chicken and saute with all the seasonings until dry.
  3. Reserve the broth from cooking the chicken to use in the pasta.

pasta

  1. To make the dough, cook the potatoes with the skins until they are soft.
  2. Then peel and knead well.
  3. Place them in a pan with salt, a tablet of chicken broth, the broth that cooked the chicken and milk.
  4. Cook everything until it boils.
  5. Once it boils, add the flour and stir until cooked.
  6. Then turn off the heat, add the margarine and stir until uniform.
  7. Put the dough in a container and allow it to cool (until you reach the point where you can put your hand in the dough) and knead until smooth.

Mounting

  1. Make three generous layers of coxinha dough in the naked cake style and between them add the chicken stuffing and also the cream cheese.
  2. To decorate, use drumstick miniatures at the top.
  3. Your cake is ready to serve!

Grades

Remembering that: if you like thinner dough, you can leave it as thick as you like. As well as the filling, you can add the amount that pleases your palate
