Potato Tower

A tower with a lot of potatoes

Ingredients

  • French fries
  • cheddar
  • Bacon
  • Milk cream
  • Sauces to taste (ketchup, mustard, etc.)

Method of Preparation

  1. Fry the chopped bacon.
  2. Fry French fries, you will need plenty of French fries.
  3. Take a pet bottle, and cut above the label, and below the label, we will use this medium to make the tower.
  4. Melt the cheddar only at the end
  5. Now it's time for you to build your tower, you have to make layers, so put potatoes in some melted cheddar and bacon and always squeeze so that the tower is firm and repeat this process a few times, until you reach the top of the tower. bottle pet.
  6. When you feel confident remove the pet bottle and if everything works out your tower will be firm and strong.
